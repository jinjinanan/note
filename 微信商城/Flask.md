



##### app_errorhandler
  Like :meth:`Flask.errorhandler` but for a blueprint.  This
        handler is used for all requests, even if outside of the blueprint.
       

| 单词       | 词性   |  词义  |
| --------   | -----:  | :----:  |
|redirect|V|重新调配|
|even if||即使|
|even if outside of the blueprint||即使此视图之外|
